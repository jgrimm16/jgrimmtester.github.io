Rikki Cup Recipe

  • Time: 20 min
  • Serves: 8 people
Ingredients Quantity
Item Amount
Chaco Chips 1 bag
Frosting 1 tub
Peanut Butter 1 Tub

Step 1: Melt and mix chocolate chips.

Step 2: Melt Peanut Butter and Frosting

Step 3: Coat cupcake cover in chocolate then place in fridge

Step 4: Remove from Fridge and place peanut butter mixture in the cupcake cup, then place back in the fridge

Step 5: Remove cups once again and add a light cover of chocolate on the top place back in fridge
